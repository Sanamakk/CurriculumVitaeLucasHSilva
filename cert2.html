Data Analyst

Data development - 2022

The Data Analyst course offered by Faculdade XP - IGTI was a comprehensive program that provided me with a strong foundation in data analysis. The course covered various topics, including data cleaning and preparation, statistical analysis, data visualization, and machine learning algorithms. The curriculum was designed to equip students with the necessary skills to succeed as a data analyst in any industry.

One of the key takeaways from the course was the importance of data cleaning and preparation. I learned how to identify and handle missing or incorrect data, which is crucial to ensuring that analysis results are accurate and reliable. Additionally, I learned how to use SQL to manage large relational databases and retrieve data efficiently.

Another crucial aspect of the course was learning about statistical analysis. I gained knowledge about the different types of statistical variables, such as categorical and continuous variables, and how to use various statistical methods to analyze and interpret data. The course also covered tools such as R, Weka, and Knime, which are widely used in the data analysis industry.

Three key topics that stood out to me as particularly important were:

  • Data Visualization: This topic taught me how to present data effectively using charts, graphs, and other visualization techniques. By creating clear and concise visuals, I could communicate complex information to stakeholders in a more accessible and understandable way.
  • Machine Learning Algorithms: This topic introduced me to machine learning algorithms and how they can be used to make predictions based on data. I learned how to apply algorithms such as decision trees, random forests, and neural networks to solve real-world problems.
  • Data Ethics: This topic explored the ethical considerations involved in handling and analyzing data. I learned about the importance of protecting personal information and ensuring that analysis results are fair and unbiased.

Overall, the Data Analyst course offered by Faculdade XP - IGTI was an excellent investment in my career development. It provided me with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed as a data analyst and gave me the confidence to tackle complex projects with ease.
